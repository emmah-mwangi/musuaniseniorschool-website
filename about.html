About Musuani Senior School

Musuani Senior School is a public Senior institution domiciled in Kenya and governed under the Basic Education Act, 2013. The school is committed to providing quality education that nurtures academic excellence, discipline, and holistic learner development.

School History

Musuani Senior School is located in Kitui County, Migwani Sub-County, Thokoa Zone. The school was officially registered on 14th March 2006 under registration number 15S300012248.

The school is categorized as an Extra County Public school established, owned, and operated by the Ministry of Education (Kenya).

Musuani Senior School operates as a day and boarding school. As at June 2025, the school had a student population of 300 learners, organized into two double streams. The school has 20 teachers, all deployed by the Teachers Service Commission (TSC).
